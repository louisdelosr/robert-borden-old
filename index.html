HOMELESS


Homeless is a choose your own adventure story, where you play as a homeless youth living in the streets of Toronto.

THE GOAL


The goal of this website is to create awareness of the homeless youth of today. Take yourself into the shoes of somebody who does not have a place to live, and learn the potential factors of what cause youth homelessness.
